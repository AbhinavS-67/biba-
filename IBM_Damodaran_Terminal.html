Damodaran Narrative-Numbers Bridge · Each claim is stress-tested against FCFF model outputs · Verified = numbers confirm narrative · Uncertain = narrative ahead of numbers
Software-First Pivot Verified
Revenue mix has shifted from 25% software in 2018 to ~45% in 2024. This directly supports the terminal margin expansion embedded in Stage 2 of the FCFF model. The numbers confirm the narrative.
AI Catalyst · watsonx Uncertain
watsonx book of business is $5.2B+, but represents <8% of total revenue. Current market price of $231.42 implies scaling to >25% within 3 years — a highly aggressive assumption not yet reflected in FCFF outputs.
Capital Intensity · Kyndryl Spin Verified
Negative working capital ($7.5B) confirms a high-quality subscription model — IBM receives cash before delivering services. S2C ratio of 0.45× is consistent with mature, efficient software firms in Damodaran's sector database.
Consulting Transformation Partial
IBM Consulting's AI-embedded backlog is growing, but margins remain compressed at ~10% vs. software's ~25%. The model assigns 9.2% near-term growth — realistic, but the mix shift risk is underweighted.
Market Pricing Premium Warning
At $231.42, the market implies a 5-year compound growth of ~18% and terminal margin of 24%+ — far above our 9.2% / 17% base case. This premium is justified only in the bull scenario where watsonx becomes a $15B+ business.
Debt Load vs. Cash Generation Monitor
Net Debt of $44.86B is substantial (3.1× EBITDA). However, IBM's stable FCF generation (~$9–11B annually) provides adequate coverage. Damodaran's framework treats this as a constraint, not a disqualifier, for mature tech firms.
Dividend Sustainability Verified
$6.67/share dividend requires ~$6.1B annually. FCF generation comfortably covers this at current growth rates. Dividend yield of 2.9% acts as a floor for price — consistent with Damodaran's "stable firm" classification criteria.
Reinvestment Rate Coherence Verified
28% reinvestment rate implies a sales-to-capital ratio of 0.45×, consistent with historical efficiency. At stable RONIC of 8% and terminal growth of 3.81%, this is mathematically coherent and does not require heroic assumptions.
